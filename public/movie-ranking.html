The Best Movies According to Greg

My top 3 movies of all time.

Fast & Furious

Fast paced, high action racing that is really well shot with a great storyline.

Rock of Ages

Funn, heatfelt musical about rock & Roll in the 80's. Featuring some great actors & singers. I love singing along to all the songs in this movie.

Serenity

Funny action packed sci-fi movie. Great storyline with some amazing special effets. This is one of my all time favourite movies.
